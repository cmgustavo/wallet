Privacy Policy

Simple Wallet is a self-custodial Bitcoin wallet designed to operate offline, ensuring user privacy and security. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

  1. No Data Collection – Simple Wallet does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data, including your wallet information and transaction history, is stored locally on your device.
  2. No Third-Party Services – Simple Wallet operates entirely offline and does not connect to any external servers or third-party services. Your Bitcoin addresses and private keys remain under your full control.
  3. Permissions – The app may request access to:
    • Camera: To scan QR codes for Bitcoin addresses.
    This access is only used locally within the app and is not shared or stored externally.
  4. Security – Since Simple Wallet is a self-custodial wallet, the security of your funds depends entirely on how you manage your recovery phrase. We strongly recommend backing up your 12-word recovery phrase securely, as losing it means losing access to your funds permanently.
